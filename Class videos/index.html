NEET Biology Syllabus

The NEET biology syllabus 2025 comprises two branches: Botany and Zoology. Botany focuses on studying plant life, while Zoology deals with animal biology. The NEET 2025 biology syllabus is designed to assess candidates on the topics covered in Class 11 and Class 12 of their school curriculum. It is important to be aware of the scope of the concepts that need to be studied to prepare for the biology section of the exam effectively. The biology syllabus for NEET 2025 is extensive, covering a wide range of topics. Therefore, one needs to understand and go through the biology NEET syllabus 2025 thoroughly.
